1. You'll receive a grid full of letters.

2. A list of 20 words to find will be provided next to the grid.

3. Search for these words in the grid.

4. Words might be hidden horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

5. Highlight at least 10 words to qualify for the game.

6. Once you've highlighted the words, put your completed paper in the box on the stage.

7. Use a highlighter to mark each word as you find it.

8. Be careful: submitting a paper with incorrect words may disqualify your team.

9. The top 3 teams to correctly and quickly spot the differences will have the chance to answer 3 questions each.

10. Each correct answer gives your team 10 points, and there's no penalty for wrong answer.
