1. Look at two pictures that look almost the same.

2. Find 10 differences between them.

3. Take your time to carefully look over both pictures.

4. Spot and point out all 10 differences.

5. Use the highlighter provided to mark these differences on the paper.

6. When you find all the differences, put your paper in the box on the stage.

7. Be careful-marking wrong differences will disqualify your team.

8. The top 3 teams to correctly and quickly spot the differences will have the chance to answer 3 questions each.

9. Each correct answer gives your team 10 points, and there's no penalty for wrong answers.
